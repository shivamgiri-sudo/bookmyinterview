# BOOK MY INTERVIEW Design System

## UI Benchmark
The visual and UX quality target is a polished interview-tech SaaS experience similar in clarity to Big Interview: clear guided journey, strong trust messaging, practice/feedback mental model, and simple onboarding. Our product adapts that benchmark for employer-side hiring: intake, assessment orchestration, candidate feedback, and interview booking.

## Brand Experience
Premium enterprise SaaS, not a generic HR portal. Visual tone: dark luxury, high contrast, glass panels, cyan/gold intelligence accents, strong data hierarchy.

## UI Principles
- Show confidence: explain AI decisions and assessment logic.
- Reduce anxiety: candidate journey must feel guided and transparent.
- High trust: compliance, audit logs, consent status, and bias safeguards visible.
- Data density without clutter: dashboards should be rich but scannable.
