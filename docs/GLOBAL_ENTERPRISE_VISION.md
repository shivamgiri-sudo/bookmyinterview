# BOOK MY INTERVIEW Global Enterprise Vision

## North Star
Build BOOK MY INTERVIEW as a global AI Hiring Operating System that helps companies convert hiring intent into assessed, explainable, interview-ready candidate pipelines across countries, roles, languages, and hiring models.

## Product Positioning
BOOK MY INTERVIEW is not a job board and not a traditional ATS. It is the intelligence layer between hiring demand and interview-ready supply.

## Global Differentiators
- Dynamic assessment orchestration by role, seniority, market, and client risk.
- Enterprise custom MCPs for JD, assessment, matching, traits, and compliance.
- Explainable candidate scorecards.
- Audio and video assessment pathways.
- Superadmin Integration Vault for global provider control.
- Compliance-first web-data acquisition through approved connectors such as Apify.
- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture.
- Human review for sensitive hiring decisions.

## Target Markets
Phase 1: India, UAE, Philippines, UK SMB/mid-market.
Phase 2: US, EU, Singapore, Australia.
Phase 3: Enterprise global deployments with SSO, regional data residency, ATS/HRMS integrations.

## Global Product Requirements
- Multi-currency billing.
- Region-aware compliance policies.
- Localized candidate experience.
- Multi-language audio assessment.
- Country-specific consent and retention settings.
- Regional storage and data residency.
- Enterprise SSO and RBAC.
- Audit-ready hiring decision records.
- Accessibility-first UI.

## Enterprise Readiness Checklist
- SOC 2 readiness plan.
- GDPR and UK GDPR data subject workflow.
- DPDP Act readiness for India.
- EEOC-conscious hiring decision controls for US expansion.
- ISO 27001-aligned security controls.
- Human-in-the-loop decisioning.
- Bias and protected attribute safeguards.
- Vendor risk management for LLMs, STT, resume parsing, and web-data providers.

## Strategic Product Moat
The moat is not just AI. The moat is the proprietary hiring intelligence graph:
- Role taxonomy
- JD gap patterns
- Skill and trait matrix
- Assessment outcomes
- Candidate evidence quality
- Interview conversion data
- Client hiring preferences
- Compliance policy decisions

This intelligence graph should improve every new hiring request.
